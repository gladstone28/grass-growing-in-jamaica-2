Zoysia Grass

Zoysia grass is a popular turfgrass known for its ability to create dense, smooth, and durable lawns. Originating from East Asia, Zoysia is highly appreciated for its tolerance to heat, drought, and heavy foot traffic, making it an excellent choice for both residential and commercial landscapes in warmer climates.

Zoysia’s slow growth rate and deep root system make it efficient in water usage and able to thrive in a variety of soil types, though it prefers sun-drenched sites. It features a fine texture and can range in color from deep green to blue-green, depending on the specific variety and environmental conditions.

While Zoysia grass requires less mowing than some other grasses due to its slow growth, it may need more time to establish initially. Proper lawn maintenance for Zoysia includes regular watering, especially during dry periods, and fertilization to ensure a lush, healthy appearance. Despite these needs, its resilience and beautiful appearance make Zoysia a favored choice for those looking to invest in their lawn’s long-term quality.
